EpilepsyPregnancy.in - Your Guide to a Healthy Pregnancy

Last Updated: February 5, 2026

Introduction

EpilepsyPregnancy.in ("we," "our," or "us") is a free educational resource for women with epilepsy who are pregnant or planning pregnancy. This website is created by Dr. Abhishek Gohel and Dr. Rutul Shah. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any information collected.

Information We Collect

This is a static educational website. We collect minimal information:

  • Analytics Data: We use privacy-focused analytics (Umami) to understand visitor patterns. This data is anonymized and contains no personal identifiers.
  • Contact Form: If you use our contact form, we collect your name, email, and message to respond to your inquiry.

We do not collect: Medical histories, pregnancy information, medication details, or any sensitive health information through this website.

Cookies

This website does not use tracking cookies. Our analytics solution is cookie-free and privacy-respecting.

How We Use Information

  • To respond to inquiries submitted through the contact form
  • To understand how visitors use the website
  • To improve educational content

Data Protection

We understand the sensitive nature of pregnancy and epilepsy-related information. Any information you share through the contact form is treated with strict confidentiality and used only to respond to your inquiry.

Medical Disclaimer

This website provides educational information only and is not a substitute for professional medical advice. Always consult your neurologist and obstetrician for advice specific to your situation. No doctor-patient relationship is established through use of this website.
